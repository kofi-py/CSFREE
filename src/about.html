about cs expert roadmap

this program is designed to be a complete, self-directed computer science education built from free, high-quality resources. the roadmap organizes learning into progressive stages with recommended projects and support resources.

mission

to democratize cs education by curating university-level materials and combining them with guidance, practice, and optional peer support.

how to use this site

  • start at stage 1 and progress through the stages sequentially, or pick a track (systems, ml, theory).
  • mark courses as complete to track progress and surface suggested projects.
